Before You Begin

📋 About the Questionnaire

This brief assessment helps us understand your current mental and emotional state so we can provide personalized support and recommendations.

15 Questions

Estimated time: 5-7 minutes

🎯 Why We Ask These Questions

  • To understand your current emotional well-being
  • To identify patterns that might affect your mental health
  • To personalize your dashboard and recommendations
  • To suggest appropriate coping strategies
  • To determine if professional support would be beneficial

🔒 Your Privacy Matters

  • All responses are confidential and encrypted
  • Your data is never shared without your consent
  • You can review or delete your data anytime
  • No question is mandatory - you can skip any question
⏭️ Can I Skip Questions?
Yes! While we recommend answering all questions for accurate personalization, you can skip any question that makes you uncomfortable. Just click the "Skip" button on each question.

💡 Tips for Best Results

  • Find a quiet, comfortable space
  • Answer honestly - there are no right or wrong answers
  • Take your time with each question
  • Think about how you've been feeling recently (past 2 weeks)

If you're experiencing a mental health emergency, please call your local crisis helpline immediately.
